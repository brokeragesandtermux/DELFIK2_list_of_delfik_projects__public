## üöÄ Key Projects

### 1. The ChatGPT Timeline Project
A 39-month retrospective tracking the shift from the launch of ChatGPT on **November 30, 2022**, to the "Reasoning Revolution" of 2026. This project documents the transition from simple generative text to complex, "System 2" autonomous agents capable of graduate-level problem-solving.

### 2. 2000 Awesome Things
Inspired by the lineage of great gratitude projects, this initiative documents **2,000 bursts of everyday magic**. From the micro-foam on a latte to the comfort of an old friend, this series acts as a training manual for the brain to notice the "undeniably right" in a complicated world.
* **Live Site:** [2000 Awesome Things](https://2000awesomethings.wordpress.com/)

### 3. The "Beautiful Sites" Paradox (*La Belaj Retejoj*)
A humorous and strategic exploration of digital aesthetics. While the main blog, *Delfik Orakle*, maintains a "digital cave" aesthetic (Blogger 2008), this project showcases modern, high-performance web design capabilities through secondary platforms.
* **Live Site:** [Fulmo.online](https://www.fulmo.online/)

### 4. Philosophy & The Joyful Chatbot (*La Gxoja Filozofio*)
A deep-dive critique of **Scholasticism, Stoicism, and Confucianism**. This project moves beyond static footnotes to create a "Joyful Philosophy," culminating in an interactive chatbot that makes ancient wisdom accessible through modern AI conversation.
* **Live Site:** [La Gxoja Filozofio](https://lagxojafilozofio.wordpress.com/)



### 5. La Gxojaj Mesagxoj Project (The "Happy Messages" Project)

Utilizing the **RALPH framework** and **KonektoCRM**, we prove that technical systems can facilitate precise communication across creative and political spheres.
